# Mosque Finder

> **Helping Muslims find nearby mosques and pray on time.**

## Why Mosque Finder Exists

Every Muslim is required to perform the five daily prayers on time.
While prayer times are widely available, finding the actual congregation
(Jamaat) time at a nearby mosque is often much harder than it should be.

Mosque Finder was created to solve that problem.

Its objective is intentionally simple:

> **Help people spend less time searching and more time praying.**

## Design Philosophy

> Solve one problem exceptionally well instead of solving many problems
> poorly.

## Core Principles

-   Accuracy over assumptions
-   Reliability over complexity
-   Community over convenience
-   Purpose over trends
-   Design around real needs

## Features

-   Nearby mosque discovery
-   Congregation timings

-   Google Maps navigation

-   Women's prayer area

-   Dashboard
-   Prayer timing management
-   Gallery
-   Announcements
-   Events

### Platform Admin

-   Mosque approvals
-   City management
-   Smart timetable importer

-   Merge & Replace imports
